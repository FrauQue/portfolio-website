Who I am

I was born and raised in Hamburg, where people greet each other with „Moin” or „Moin Moin“ – if they are communicative.

From contributing to the school newspaper to developing my own fanzine, flyer and poster for concerts, my enthusiasm for designing began.

After an intensive and beautiful volunteer year in a facility for disabled people, I decided to study communication design to pursue my love for design. I also co-founded „Viva con Agua“ in 2006 to continue working for a better world. After many years in print, I built up the BILD branch office in Los Angeles in 2013/2014 (BILD? Yeah, I know, however, it is still the highest circulated daily newspaper in Germany and I am thankful for all the experience I gained during my time there) and switched to the digital part of the brand to get closer to my affinity for technology.

My professional knowledge is based on 15 years of experience as a Communication Designer mostly in the crazy german newspaper business, including positions such as Editor Layout & Editorial Design and Editor Layout & Web Design at bild.de. Nevertheless, things change, and I decided to take a step and focus more on my passion to develop user-centric products.

This led me to where I am now: being an enthusiastic UX Designer with knowledge (always expanding), experience, curiosity as well as strategic thinking and communication skills who love seeing an idea evolve into a comprehensive and holistic product which makes life better.

I would describe myself as a craft designer, because a design has to put its actual function in the foreground should be pleasant to use and still be beautiful to look at. That’s why I'm constantly thinking about everyday improvements, even at the most inconvenient times (sleep Frauke, sleep!) and in unusual situations (with my hands in the garden soil). I always notice room for improvement and want to think about and implement them. Not every problem can be solved with Duct Tape!

My favourite ways to reset myself are (watching) soccer, skiing, horseback riding and scuba diving, when I have the opportunity and time. Check out my CV for more information and of course, I can also send you my graphic design portfolio via email should you want it. Just drop me a line.

If we work together, you’ll never walk alone.
